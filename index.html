Tuesday Nights, 7pm.

We gather weekly on Tuesday nights from 7:00 pm to 8:45 pm and hangout, play, worship God through music, hear God’s Word taught, and gather in small groups to wrestle with what God is teaching us. We also connect outside of Tuesdays to go deeper–to share our lives together and walk together as we walk with Jesus.

About.

Trailhead Students is the student ministry (9th thru 12th grade) of Trailhead Church. We like to hang out, have fun together, and learn and grow as followers of Jesus. We invite Christians and non-Christians to come hang out with us and learn more about Jesus.

We want to see students turn away from their rebellion against God, what the Bible calls “sin,” and turn towards him to receive forgiveness and new life in Jesus. And we want to see students live radically transformed lives for Jesus–becoming like him, and telling others about him.

We meet on Tuesday nights from 7:00 pm to 8:45 pm at Trailhead Church:

Meet Brian.

Brian is a former high school History teacher. He spent five years in the education system before being hired as our full-time Student Ministry Director. He has a passion for seeing students turn outward in their lives, living for Jesus rather than themselves. He is helped by his wife Melinda. Melinda works as a social worker within a local junior high. Together, they desire to see students meet Jesus.
